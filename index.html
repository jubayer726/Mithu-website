About Us

Vesture Global Sourcing is a 100% export-oriented Garments Manufacturing Buying House with a highly integrated vertical setup with all kinds of garments items to cater a flexible & committed service to its customers, assuring the perfect blend of quality & efficiency.

Our Mission

To provide our customers a high-quality product with an excellent service which conform and surpasses the customer expectations. Our focus is on maintaining an innovation-centric corporate culture to promote and develop the efficiency of our each employee and maximize the company's capability to meet the exact needs of our customers

Our Vision

To become the benchmark manufacturing organization Buying Office of the RMG sector all over the World by sustaining the organizational excellence through visionary leadership and innovative efforts to develop our product quality.
